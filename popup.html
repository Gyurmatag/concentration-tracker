About the Concentration Experiment

Welcome to our TED concentration tracking experiment! üß†

What is this?

This is a community science experiment to understand how long people can maintain focus on tasks without getting distracted.

How to participate:

  1. Start: Click "Start Focus" when you begin a task
  2. Focus: Work on your task without checking notifications, emails, or social media
  3. Stop: Click "Stop" when you get distracted or finish
  4. Send: Submit your data anonymously to help our research

Privacy:

All data is collected anonymously. We only track timing information, no personal details.

Purpose:

This research helps us understand attention patterns and could lead to better productivity tools and insights about digital wellness.
